Job Openings

Assistant editor, opinion

Send us your CV: jobs@newstoday.com

In this role you will support the opinion editor in commissioning, editing and producing the opinion section of the News Today website. The assistant opinion editor will produce lively and varied content that is interesting and important, swiftly and accurately edit and sub pieces, nurture relationships with regular contributors, generate comment ideas that are intelligent and original, and deputise for the opinion editor and deputy opinion editor as required.

Deputy editor, lifestyle

Send us your CV: jobs@newstoday.com

In this role you will support the lifestyle editor in commissioning, editing and writing copy for the lifestyle section of the News Today website, including stories on food, fashion, health and wellbeing, relationships, travel and sustainable living. The deputy lifestyle editor will also commission freelancers and source new talent, edit copy and sub freelance pieces, originate timely and lively human interest and news ideas that can be adopted across multiple platforms, liaise with the picture and multimedia desks to ensure stories are visually engaging, assist with special projects and forward planning, and deputise for the lifestyle editor as required.

Live blogger/ reporter

Send us your CV: jobs@newstoday.com

The live blogger/reporter will set up and anchor live blogs on set-piece or breaking news events as directed by news editors. This role will also use reporting skills to verify developments and interview key players in live stories, write standalone news stories when required, gather and contextualise contributions from News Today correspondents in the field, in-house analysts and external media outlets, monitor external sources for latest developments and engage with social media to promote stories and uncover information.
