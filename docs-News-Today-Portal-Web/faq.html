FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find the most frequently asked questions and their answers!

What can I find on News Today websites?

News Today delivers the latest news from your community and the world. For a top news that we serve, click on our 'Home' button.

Which browser should I be using to get the most from your site?

It is optimised for versions 4 and above of Netscape and Internet Explorer. If your browser can't display tables or Java, use the text only version.

How do I know if I am viewing the most up-to-date material?

Click the refresh or reload button on your browser every time you visit.

What's the best way of hearing audio and viewing video?

Download RealPlayer version 9 or above from RealNetworks or Microsoft Windows Media Player version 9 or above to get multimedia capability.

How can I see news that is relevant to my country?

Our goal is to offer an app that curates relevant news for users all over the world, and from all walks of life. To see news more relevant to your country, you can follow more news sources based in your country, including smaller, local sources.

I have a suggestion. How do I give it to you?

Please send us your feedback at our support email below. Developers regularly review feedback to improve the app experience. We thank you in advance for your suggestions!
